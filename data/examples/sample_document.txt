CONFIDENTIAL MEMO

From: John Smith, CEO
To: Board of Directors
Date: January 15, 2024
Subject: Proposed Merger with TechVentures Inc

Dear Board Members,

I am writing to formally propose a strategic merger between ACME Corporation and TechVentures Inc, headquartered in San Francisco, California.

Background:
TechVentures Inc was founded in 2018 by Jane Doe and Michael Chen. The company has grown to 250 employees and generated $50 million in revenue last year. Their primary office is located at 123 Market Street, San Francisco, CA 94102.

Key Personnel:
- Jane Doe (CEO, TechVentures Inc): Former VP of Engineering at Google
- Michael Chen (CTO, TechVentures Inc): MIT graduate, 15 years experience
- Sarah Johnson (CFO, TechVentures Inc): Previously at Goldman Sachs

Proposed Transaction:
ACME Corporation would acquire TechVentures Inc for $500 million in a combination of cash and stock. The transaction would be structured as follows:
- $300 million cash payment
- $200 million in ACME stock

Meeting Schedule:
A preliminary meeting was held on December 10, 2023, at the Marriott Hotel in New York City. Attendees included:
- John Smith (ACME Corporation)
- Robert Williams (ACME Corporation, CFO)
- Jane Doe (TechVentures Inc)
- David Brown (Legal Counsel, representing TechVentures)

Next Steps:
The due diligence team, led by Emily Davis from McKinsey & Company, will conduct a thorough review over the next 60 days. Legal documentation is being prepared by Wilson & Partners LLP.

A final decision meeting is scheduled for March 20, 2024, at ACME headquarters in Boston, Massachusetts.

Financial Projections:
The combined entity is projected to generate $750 million in annual revenue by 2025, with operations spanning offices in:
- Boston, Massachusetts (ACME HQ)
- San Francisco, California (TechVentures HQ)
- Austin, Texas (new development center)
- London, United Kingdom (international expansion)
